Luca Colella

Manager

I help my clients stand out and they help me grow.

With over 25 years of international experience in the basketball realm spanning Italy, the UK, and the UAE, Luca Colella serves as the Operations Director and Manager of Dubai Stealers Academy. Holding certifications as a USAB certified coach and Basketball England certified table official, his journey in this sport has been diverse and enriching

Luca's profound passion for basketball transcends words, driving him to impart invaluable skills and lessons to young athletes. He finds immense joy in coaching, especially working one-on-one with clients, fostering a deep connection through the shared love for the game. As a driving force behind Dubai Stealers Academy, Luca is dedicated to instilling a lifelong appreciation for basketball and helping individuals reach their full potential.
