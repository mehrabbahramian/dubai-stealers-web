WHO WE ARE?

DubaiStealers is a Dubai sports council licensed academy, stands as a beacon of excellence in basketball education within the UAE. Committed to fueling passion and nurturing young athletes, we proudly showcase three USAB-certified coaches, including a professional player, who enrich our training programs.

OUR MISSIONS!

Catering to ages 5 to 16, our academy prioritizes skill development, teamwork values, and respect for fellow basketball players, coaches, and referees. Our basketball ASA clubs and youth camps offer a harmonious blend of enjoyment and reward, creating a relaxed yet enriching atmosphere. Dive into our personalized 1-on-1 training sessions and clinics, which delve deep into specific game aspects, offering immersive training opportunities.
